LANGUAGE CAMPS

It is a great opportunity to unlock the world of language and culture at summer language
camps for teens in South Dublin! By joining these camps students will have a one-of-a-kind
immersion experience that will help them improve their language skills while having fun.
Experienced teachers and interactive activities will help teens to improve their speaking, listening,
and comprehension skills in French, Spanish, German and Chinese.
